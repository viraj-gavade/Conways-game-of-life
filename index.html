What is Conway's game of life?

Conway's game of life was a game made by the mathematician John Horton Conway. Conway's game of life is a cellular automation used to represent the following rules:

Any live cell with less than 2 live neighbors die

Any live cell with over 3 live neighbors die

Any dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors become alive
